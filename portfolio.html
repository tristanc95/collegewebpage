Collaborations

What are collaborations?

In the sense that I am bringing up with the community I am a part of, a collaboration is whenever usually a host or co-hosts will gather up participants and select individual parts to make for a full song or custom made medley. These projects can span from being done in the short time of a month to some taking over a year! I have participated in many of these projects (well over 50 at this point) and have also hosted/co-hosted several collabs that have become majorly successfully in the community's terms of success.


I'd like to share some of them with you now.
