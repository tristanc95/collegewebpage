Oh okay! Tell me more about yourself.

Ever since I was born, I've been raised and lived in Corpus Christi, TX; particularly in a little suburban area called Calallen. Located closer to Nueces County, Calallen is where I've spent 22 years of my life growing up and becoming who I am today. I graduated Calallen High School in 2014, 31st in my class and ended up going to TAMUK because of how close it was to family and the good prices it had to offer. There really isn't that much to do in Calallen so most of the time people will just take the 30 minute drive into Corpus Christi, or just hang around some parks or playgrounds that are around. It's a nice little town with really not a lot going on, but not enough to be considered a ghost town.

I now reside up in Fort Worth, TX and work at the UNT Health Science Center as a IT Specialist, but I'm always looking for new opportunities in not only the IT Tech field, but even some audio and video fields as well! I know I'm an IT guy by trade, but I've also been doing video and sound work for several years that you can read about here and here!
