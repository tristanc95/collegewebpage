Video Editing/Remixes

Over the past 6 years, I've been partaking in a niche little community that deals with editing and creating videos out of things such as films, tv shows, other YouTube videos, etc. At first, I started out as a general remixer; trying my best to make things what some would consider "trippy". However, as of 2013, I moved over into another subsection of said community that does the same thing, but edits it all to music and creates visuals for said remixes. In short, we watch various pieces of media, find some sort of vocal or percussive sound that could match the instrument used in the song, and then pitch them to the song itself. After doing this, we will put some visuals to the work to make it much more visually interesting to watch or just to showcase what the sample playing actually is. Since 2011, I have been learning and teaching myself how to use various video and audio editors to make these kinds of videos which has taught me quite a bit about multimedia as a whole. Doing this has not only been such an experience and something that I still love to do, but has helped me a lot in terms of learning about computers, video editing and videography, music creation, editing, music theory, and many more. I've also learned some life skills from these videos such as project management from hosting collaborations with upwards of 30+ people, and even more.
